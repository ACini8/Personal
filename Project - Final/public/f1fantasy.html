What is F1 Fantasy?

F1 Fantasy is a game available on the Formula 1 app, which allows you to pick 5 drivers and 2 teams with a budget of 100 million euros. Now depending on how these drivers do in a said grand prix weekend, you will get points correlating to their performance.

Last year over 1.5 million users participated, and the grand winner gets awesome prizes such as 2x Paddock Pass Weekend Tickets to any Grand Prix od the winner's choosing! You can create mini leagues with your friends and compete with one another, or you can join the TGPN Mini-League where the winner will get a £50 gift card to the F1 gift shop!
